Dear Reader —

What we collect, why we collect it, and what stays yours.

Information We Collect

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

Any third-party service providers are only permitted to use your information to perform services on our behalf.

Article IV

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate the website, remember preferences, and understand how visitors use the Service. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work properly as a result.

Article VI

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under state privacy laws — such as the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), the Utah Consumer Privacy Act (UCPA), or similar laws in other states — including the right to:

The Service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us at buy@homefrontmarkets.com and we will delete it promptly.
