▸ About Us

The heart of our country is freedom – and, with it, the obligation to choose well. As Americans, we are bound together by the freedoms we share and protect.

Choosing American – even at a premium – is among the most meaningful acts an individual can take for their home, for their community, and, ultimately, for our great nation.

In a world demanding and incentivizing dependence on business overseas, our shared sense of country, community, and self has drifted. We live more isolated lives, unaware of and unappreciative of the value we offer one another. We sacrifice quality and sustainability for what is convenient and accessible, and often without even knowing it. This silent drift from choices that strengthen our country and fellow countrymen has and will eventually weaken both. We must consciously choose a better path.

Homefront Markets is committed to empowering such a choice on every front.

What we stand for

01

We stand for a deeper rooted local community and a shared American patriotism.

02

We stand for an unapologetic national and local pride – a pride that moves us to celebrate the founders, companies, and products that honor it and to denounce those that betray it, always in the spirit of hope for what America is meant to be.

03

We stand for the central unit of all healthy countries: the family. Fostering a local and national environment that supports and promotes the family is of greatest importance to us.

04

We stand for the liberated Buyer and the fair Maker. Honest business begins with honest information, which is why we strive to educate the American buyer as they exercise virtuous citizenship.

To help both ends of American commerce, we take our responsibility to inform with seriousness and urgency. We publish a bi-weekly newsletter to share all of the stories, founders, companies, and products from across the nation that are missing from your newsfeed or algorithms. Our company seeks information from our subscribers about local talent. We investigate business that is worthy of wider recognition and promotion. Through our newsletter and digital marketplace, we hope to empower American makers everywhere. We want to help new small businesses to flourish. Through these efforts, we aim to reach interested buyers in a space that builds trust in quality domestic products. We continuously push to expand our impact through local and national events and outreach. At Homefront Markets, we will always strive to keep America working.

A revival of American Commerce is where we rekindle patriotic trust. It is within our reach to live in cities and towns where neighbors and fellow citizens become the support we rely on to sustain and provide for our families. As we come together to promote our communities and our shared national interest, we will create a place of trust and fellowship. The roots of a deepened cultural heritage are forever connected to The American Marketplace.

So join us as we sustain the American market. Discover a deeper appreciation for your local, regional, and national community. Subscribe and learn with us about all that the American men and women of this great country have to offer.
